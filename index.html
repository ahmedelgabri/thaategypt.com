Thaat Social Enterprise & Innovative Consultancy

We seek smaller numbers of beneficiaries at each initiative because at Thaat we want to influence the way our beneficiaries handle their craft and design businesses.

We will also focus on expanding our craft/design and business related industries networks-that are significant in supporting long-term sustainable social, cultural and economic benefits.

Thaat is the brainchild of Peri A. Abou Zied, who prefers to be described as someone who holds a lamp over her head rather than a social entrepreneur. Peri is based in Cairo, Egypt and holds a MFA in Outsiders art and an MA. in Social Entrepreneurship from Goldsmiths college in London.
